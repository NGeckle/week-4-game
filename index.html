Gem Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 gems below. By clicking on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game if your total score matches the random number, but if your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

The value of each gem is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the games starts, the values of each gem will change.

Click the "Start" button to start.
